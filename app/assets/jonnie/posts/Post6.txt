<p>Meet your new best friend. Have you ever made a service call the the server and have no results, but yet you didn't receive a error, and you were just sitting there thinking, what the hell is going on? Why isn't my data showing up? Well I have, all to often.</p>  <p>Let me introduce you to a little guy named &amp;lt;mx:TraceTarget /&amp;gt;, I love using this tag in my main application whenever I am interacting with the server.
